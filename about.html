About Me

I am a devoted follower of the Pushti Bhakti Marg, the path of divine grace established by Shri Mahaprabhuji (Shri Vallabhacharyaji). I believe that Shuddhadvaita Vedanta is not merely a philosophical doctrine, but a timeless way of life that nurtures inner harmony, compassion, and universal peace.

Heritage & Guidance

I am honored to belong to the 16th generation of the lineage of Shri Puranmalji, the eleventh son of Veer Shiromani Maharana Pratap Singhji. Alongside this ancestral legacy, I have the privilege of receiving the spiritual guidance of my revered Sadguru. The union of courage inherited from my lineage and devotion inspired by my spiritual path continues to shape my character and strengthens my resolve in every aspect of life.
