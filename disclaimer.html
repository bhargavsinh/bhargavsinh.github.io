ARTICLE I — DEFINITIONS

1.1 “Infrastructure” refers to the digital ecosystem hosted at bhargavsinh.github.io including all source code, writings, digital archives, visual works and documentation.

1.2 “Author” refers to Sisodiya Bhargavsinh Mahendrasinh, creator and legal proprietor of the said digital infrastructure.

1.3 “User” means any individual, organisation, automated crawler or artificial intelligence system accessing the website.

ARTICLE II — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

2.1 All original works on this platform are protected under the Copyright Act, 1957 and the Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works.

2.2 The Author retains full moral and economic rights over the intellectual material unless explicitly stated otherwise.

2.3 Limited reproduction may be permitted under fair dealing provisions for research, scholarship, criticism, or reporting.

ARTICLE III — ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE GOVERNANCE

3.1 Automated scraping, mass downloading, or structured data extraction from this platform is not authorised without written consent.

3.2 Training machine learning models or generative AI systems using the proprietary content requires explicit authorisation.

3.3 Robots directives embedded in the website infrastructure must be respected by automated agents.

ARTICLE IV — DATA PROTECTION

4.1 Personal data voluntarily shared by users shall be processed in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

4.2 The Author shall act as the Data Fiduciary for information collected directly through this website.

ARTICLE V — ACADEMIC FREEDOM

5.1 Historical commentary and genealogical documentation published herein constitute scholarly research protected under Article 19(1)(a) of the Constitution of India.

ARTICLE VI — LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

6.1 The platform is provided on an “AS IS” basis without guarantees of completeness or accuracy.

6.2 The Author shall not be liable for indirect damages resulting from use of this website.

ARTICLE VII — USER OBLIGATIONS

7.1 Users shall refrain from malicious activity including hacking, vulnerability probing, or identity impersonation.

ARTICLE VIII — GOVERNING LAW

8.1 This Charter shall be governed by the laws of the Republic of India.

8.2 Courts located at Lunawada, District Mahisagar, Gujarat shall exercise exclusive jurisdiction.

ARTICLE IX — SEVERABILITY

If any provision of this Charter is declared invalid, remaining provisions shall remain legally effective.
