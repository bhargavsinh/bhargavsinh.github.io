Grievance Redressal Policy

The Digital Estate of Bhargavsinh Sisodiya is committed to maintaining the highest standards of digital integrity, historical accuracy, and ethical compliance. If any user, individual, or entity has legitimate concerns regarding the content, usage, copyrights, or any matter related to this website, they may submit a formal grievance for our review.

10.1 Detailed Submission Process

To ensure a swift and accurate resolution, all grievances must be submitted in a structured written format. The submission must explicitly include:

  • Specific Identification: The exact URL, page name, or section where the issue is located.
  • Nature of the Issue: A clear, comprehensive description of what the concern is (e.g., historical inaccuracy, copyright claim, privacy concern).
  • Legal or Factual Basis: Valid reasoning, historical proof, or legal documentation supporting your claim.
  • Complainant Details: Your full legal name, contact number, physical address, and a valid email address.
  • Declaration: A statement that the information provided in the complaint is accurate and made in good faith.

10.2 Comprehensive Review Mechanism

Once a formal grievance is received, it undergoes a meticulous review process:

  • Acknowledgment: We aim to acknowledge the receipt of your grievance within 48 to 72 business hours.
  • Investigation: All grievances are reviewed objectively and in good faith. We may cross-reference historical archives, code repositories, or legal guidelines.
  • Timeframe: A formal response or resolution will typically be provided within 15 to 30 working days, depending on the complexity of the matter.

10.3 Resolution Approach & Actions

Upon concluding the review, the Author reserves the sole discretion to take appropriate action, which may include:

  • Clarification: Providing additional context or historical background to the complainant.
  • Correction/Removal: Making necessary edits, amending digital records, or removing the content if the grievance is found to be justified and legally binding.
  • Rejection: Dismissing the grievance with a clear explanation if it lacks merit, proof, or violates our terms of service.

10.5 Strict Misuse & Defamation Clause

The grievance mechanism is established to address genuine concerns. Frivolous, abusive, repetitive, or malicious complaints designed to harass, defame, or disrupt the Digital Estate will be summarily ignored. The Author reserves the right to take stringent legal action, including claiming damages for defamation, under the applicable Information Technology and Penal laws against individuals misusing this policy.
