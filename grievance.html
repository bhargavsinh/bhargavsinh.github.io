10. Grievance Redressal

If any user, individual, or entity has concerns regarding content, usage, or any matter related to this website, they may submit a formal grievance.

10.1 Submission Process

  • Clearly mention the specific content or issue
  • Provide valid reasoning or legal basis (if applicable)
  • Include your name and contact details

10.2 Review Process

  • All grievances will be reviewed in good faith
  • A response may be provided within a reasonable timeframe (typically 15–30 days)

10.3 Resolution Approach

The Author may, at discretion:

  • Provide clarification
  • Make necessary corrections (if justified)
  • Reject the grievance with explanation
